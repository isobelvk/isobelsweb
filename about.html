    so u wanna learn about me?

    "my name isoooobel / married to myself / my love isobel / living by herself"

    if you know those lyrics... i think we might be good friends. i was named after that song!!

    anyway, i think you know my name already. the most defining things about me is that i'm a creative at heart. i love to write music, journal, draw, eat delicious food, workout, and play video games. all my life, i've always been making things. my latest project is this website. my primary project, however, will always be music. it's a bit of an obsession and i'm very much an obsessive person.

    here are some things i've been obsessed with lately:
    • learning to code
    • my girlfriend
      • ithe feeling of new guitar strings
    • idm music
    • poetry

    so what made me want to code a website?

    well... i guess i long for a time before the internet was infested with cororpate greed and spam. by extension i'm interested in the dead internet theory (there's a lot of crap if you look into it, so minus all that). in short, i believe we should revive the personal web. it's a unlimited avenue for self expression, unlike the formatting and customization boundaries that social media posits. bring back the old web! i also believe in creative world building and want a space to expand upon my music.
