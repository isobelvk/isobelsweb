laundry

[chorus] time has been passing by me
have i told you it’s exhausting?
living life just to do my laundry
got this burdened body, wish you’d wash it off me
at the time of writing this, i was very dissatisfied with life. it's hard to say i'm satisfied even now. but that's what laundry is about. every week felt like rinse and repeat, which is a feeling i often circle back to usually come january.
and every night, i start sinking deeper
drowning in this dark demeanor
tell myself that i’m just a dreamer
got no bark, no bite
i’m no golden retriever
sometimes i feel like i'm all talk and no show. but maybe i'm not even talk, hence "no bark, no bite." i actually intended on changing that golden retriever line but i decided not to stray from the original demo. i wrote the entire thing in a haste.

[verse]
and it’s so cynical, cyclical, washing up my typical
crimson lips, sour kiss, afraid i’ve had enough of this
crimson lips = facade, sour kiss = bitter reality. this line is about being a little disillusioned with life.
i want to be left alone
my life is lived just for show
so can i burn this body?
can you wash it for me?
will you do my laundry
when i’m turning twenty?
this lyric highlights the anticipation of turning 20 and the responsibilities that age entails. maybe you feel like you're outgrowing the care you receive in life, and maybe you still crave it.
why would you want to want to want me
when i don’t really want me?
g-got a little lazy
g-got a little bit older
still need a shoulder
i miss being your big sister
i really, really miss her
another lyric i wanted to change because i felt like it sounded a little corny... but sometimes corny gets the message across best. sometimes i feel so wrapped up in myself that i don't show up for the people i care about, like my little sister.
